About me

I am a researcher who loves exploring domains like wireless communication, information theory, estimation theory, Radar sensing, AR, VR, Haptic feedback, and Machine learning.
In 2015, I started my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication at the National Institute of Engineering, Mysuru.(NIE) I was also fortunate to work for several organizations as Research and Development intern for nearly 33 months during my bachelor's.
After completing my bachelor's, I joined MMRFIC as Digital Signal Processing System Engineer. I worked for over a year on domains like Beamforming, GPS Rx algorithm design in the presence of Jamming signals, Gold Sequence, Foutain Codes.
In late 2020 I joined IIT, Kanpur as a Senior Project Associate (5G indigenous Testbed) for developing 5G physical layer algorithms for sub-6 and mm-wave. Click here to know more
In 2021, I started my master's degree at the University of Southern California, Los Angeles. Along with my master's, I am also working as Research Assistant at WiDes Lab.
