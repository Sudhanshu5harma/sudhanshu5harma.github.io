About

Sudhanshu was born and raised in India. He received his Bachelor's degree in Electronics and communication engineering in 2019 from the National Institute of Engineering, Mysore(NIE). During his Bachelor's, he worked for nearly three years as a research and development intern where he mainly developed Embedded System design for AR/VR technologies.
After completing his bachelor's in 2019, he joined MMRFIC as Digital Signal Processing System Engineer and worked on developing Beamforming techniques, Error-correcting codes, and GPS receiver algorithms.
In late 2020 he joined IIT, Kanpur as a Senior Project Associate (5G indigenous Testbed) for developing 5G physical layer algorithms for sub-6 and mm-wave.
In 2021, He started his master's degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California, Los Angeles. At USC he is specializing in Wireless Communication and Signal Processing. Here at USC, he is also associated with WIDES Lab working as Research Assistant on two NSF projects. One is related to Joint communication and Sensing, and another one is developing low-power terahertz.
